LEGAL & COMPLIANCE

Privacy Policy

How we protect your personal and property information during your transition to solar energy.

1. Information We Collect

When you request a site survey, contact us via WhatsApp, or proceed with a solar installation, we may collect personal information including your Name, Phone Number, Email Address, Physical Property Address, and copies of your recent Electricity Bills (specifically JVVNL/AVVNL) required for load calculation.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information exclusively to provide rooftop solar services. This includes:

  • Scheduling and conducting 3D site surveys at your property.
  • Calculating your energy generation requirements based on historic utility bills.
  • Processing official paperwork for your JVVNL net-metering approvals.
  • Filing your application on the National Portal for the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana subsidy.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to telemarketers or third-party advertising agencies. Your information is only shared securely with necessary official entities to complete your solar project. These include the State Electricity Board (for net metering), the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (for subsidy disbursal), and Tata Power Solaroof (for component registration and 25-year performance warranties).

4. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Documents such as property deeds, ID proofs, and electricity bills submitted for government subsidy processing are handled with strict confidentiality and used solely for their intended official purpose.

5. Contact Us
