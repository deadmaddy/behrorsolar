Terms & Conditions

Service agreements for rooftop solar installations, PM Surya Ghar subsidies, and Net Metering.

1. General Agreement

By engaging Behror Solar (operating under Global Green Energy, Alwar) for a site survey, consultation, or solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) installation, you agree to the terms outlined below. These terms are designed to ensure transparency regarding government policies, utility board regulations, and installation timelines.

2. Quotes and Site Surveys

Any pricing, capacity estimates, or generation forecasts provided over the phone or via WhatsApp are strictly preliminary. Final, binding quotations are only provided after a physical or 3D site survey is completed. The final price may vary based on roof height, shading, custom HDG structure requirements, and the exact length of AC/DC cabling required to reach your meter box.

3. PM Surya Ghar Subsidy Processing

As an authorized vendor, Behror Solar will facilitate and submit all necessary documentation on the National Portal for the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana on your behalf. However, please note:

  • The subsidy is paid directly to the customer's linked bank account by the government, not deducted upfront from our invoice.
  • Behror Solar cannot control the exact timeline of the subsidy disbursal, which is determined entirely by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE).
  • Customers are responsible for providing accurate, active bank account details seeded with Aadhaar as required by the National Portal.

4. JVVNL / State Discom Net Metering

We provide end-to-end liaison services for your net metering approval with JVVNL/AVVNL. Installation of the bidirectional meter is subject to local AEN/JEN approvals, technical feasibility, and local distribution transformer capacity. Behror Solar is not liable for delays caused by state electricity board processing times, utility strikes, or grid infrastructure limitations in your specific locality.

5. Warranties & Maintenance

Product warranties (e.g., 25-year performance warranty on solar modules, 5-year or 10-year warranties on inverters) are provided directly by the original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) such as Tata Power Solaroof, Premier Energies, etc. Behror Solar provides a standard workmanship warranty for the physical installation. Regular cleaning of the panels (recommended every 10–15 days due to local dust conditions) is the responsibility of the system owner.

6. Cancellations & Material Orders

Once a project contract is signed and the initial advance is paid, materials (including custom-fabricated Hot-Dip Galvanized structures) are ordered immediately. Cancellations after material procurement has begun may be subject to restocking or fabrication fees deducted from the advance payment.
