Common questions from Neemrana customers

Deciding between on-grid, hybrid or off-grid? Our solar system comparison guide explains each option with local context.

How quickly can you reach my property in Neemrana?

Our base in Behror is roughly 20 kilometres — about a 20-minute drive — from most of Neemrana town and the surrounding rural areas. Site surveys are typically scheduled within a day of enquiry, and after-sales callouts don't require waiting on a distant regional office.

Do you install solar for industrial units in the Neemrana NIA / industrial area?

Yes. Neemrana's industrial belt includes a wide range of manufacturing sheds with large, often metal-sheet rooftops and high daytime load profiles. These systems are engineered differently from residential installations — typically higher-capacity arrays with industrial-grade inverters and structures designed for the specific roof type and load pattern of the facility.

Is PM Surya Ghar subsidy available for Neemrana Rural properties?

Yes, residential consumers in Neemrana Rural with a valid JVVNL domestic connection are eligible on the same terms as urban Neemrana applicants. The subsidy is ₹30,000 per kW for systems up to 2 kW, rising to a capped ₹78,000 for systems of 3 kW and above. Properties on agricultural or commercial tariffs need a separate eligibility check before applying.

Which discom handles net metering for Neemrana — and how long does approval take?

Neemrana falls under JVVNL (Jaipur Vidyut Vitran Nigam Limited). After installation, we submit the net metering application directly to JVVNL and coordinate the technical inspection on your behalf. Approval timelines vary with JVVNL's processing load, but we follow up directly so you don't have to chase the paperwork yourself.
